About

nsukuzonke

Welcome to my site. I am Peter Ndlovu, also known as 'Nsukuzonke' and 'The Flying Elephant'. I am professional football manager with more than 30 years' experience at the highest level of the game. Born in the great city of Bulawayo, I am the first black African to play in the English Premier League, and the all-time leading scorer for the Zimbabwe national team, the Warriors, of which I was captain and highest-capped player.

Today my legacy in football continues through my work as the team manager of the 2016 African football champions, Mamelodi Sundowns (South Africa), where I also played as a midfielder; I also serve as a FIFA Ambassador; president of the Zimbabwe Warriors Trust; chairman of the Peter Ndlovu Foundation; and as a consultant for FIFA-designated high-level projects.

If you are an individual or organization seeking a consultant with a total understanding of the football landscape in Africa, do not hesitate to get in touch: I am ready to work productively and professionally with you.
